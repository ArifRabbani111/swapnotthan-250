About Us

Our Story

Swapnotthan was founded in 2010 with just 10 passionate volunteers who wanted to make a difference in their community. Over the years, our organization has grown significantly, expanding to three dedicated departments and now serving over 5000 people annually.

Our Mission & Vision

To create a compassionate society where every child has access to education, healthcare, and opportunities to thrive. We envision a future where no child is left behind due to economic disadvantages.

Compassion

About Swapnotthan

We are a voluntary charity organization based at Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, dedicated to helping disadvantaged children in our community.

Our Journey

2010

Foundation

Swapnotthan was founded with 10 volunteers at Shahjalal University of Science and Technology.

2013

First Major Campaign

Launched our first major fundraising campaign for underprivileged children's education.

2016

Expansion

Expanded our operations to include Blood Wing and Education Wing as separate departments.

2020

Growth

Grew to three departments serving over 5000 people annually despite pandemic challenges.

2023

Digital Transformation

Launched our new website and digital platforms to reach more beneficiaries and volunteers.
